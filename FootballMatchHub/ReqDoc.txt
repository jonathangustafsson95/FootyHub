FootballMatchHub is a mini social network hub that makes it easy for football players to add and track matches played.

Players can sign up and lsit their matches. When adding a match, they should specify date/time, location, opponent team name, team name, goals, assist and so on.

A player should have a My Matches page, from where they should be able to add, edit or remove matches.

Players should be able to view and search all matches and view details.

Players should be able to follow other players and get notifications on any news.
